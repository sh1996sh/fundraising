Our Story of Survival

My name is Hassan Abu Shamla, and I'm reaching out to you from Gaza during one of the most devastating periods in our history. The war that began in 2023 has completely upended our lives, and we desperately need your help to survive.

🏠 Our Home is Destroyed

Our family home, where we built our dreams and memories, has been completely destroyed by the ongoing conflict. We lost everything - our furniture, clothes, documents, and the safe haven we once had.

💰 Complete Loss of Income

I lost my job when the war began, and there are no employment opportunities available. We have no source of income to provide for our children's basic needs like food, medicine, and shelter.

🏕️ Living in a Tent

Currently, our family of 5 is living in a small tent in a displacement camp. We face extreme weather conditions, lack of privacy, and constant fear for our safety.
